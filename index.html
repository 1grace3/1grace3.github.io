Tom Hiddleston

Actor ● Producer ● Soundtrack Artist

Tom Hiddleston is an English actor. He gained international fame portraying Loki in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), starting with Thor in 2011 and most recently in Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania in 2023, additionally headlining the Disney+ series Loki in 2021.
